## Concept

Including a handful of worked examples directly in the prompt so the model infers the desired pattern from demonstration rather than from a description of the rule — a direct application of [[Show Don't Tell]] to prompt design.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Prompt Engineering]], usually layered on top of [[System Prompt Design]], and an alternative to [[Chain of Thought Prompting]] when the desired output format matters more than the reasoning path.
