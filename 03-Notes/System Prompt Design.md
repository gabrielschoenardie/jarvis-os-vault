## Concept

The system prompt sets a model's persistent role, constraints, and tone for a whole session — the CLAUDE.md-style instructions in this vault are a system-prompt-adjacent mechanism, loaded once and applied to every response.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Prompt Engineering]], and often combined with [[Few-Shot Examples]] to show the desired behavior rather than only describe it, and with [[Chain of Thought Prompting]] when the task needs visible reasoning.
