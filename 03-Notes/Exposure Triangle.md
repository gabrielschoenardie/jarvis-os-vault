## Concept

The three controls that determine a photo's exposure — aperture, shutter speed, and ISO — trade off against each other: opening any one up to gain light means another must close down to keep exposure constant.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Photography]], foundational to [[Composition Rules]] since depth-of-field choices depend on aperture, and to [[Color Grading]] which shapes an image after exposure has already been captured.
