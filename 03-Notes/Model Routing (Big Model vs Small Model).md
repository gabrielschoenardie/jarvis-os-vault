## Concept

Choosing which model handles which part of a task — a larger, more capable model for design and judgment calls, a smaller or faster model for high-volume execution — rather than running everything through a single model tier.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — AI Models]], and the technical basis for [[Big Model Designs, Small Model Executes]] in [[Hub — Principles]]. Also bounded by the [[Model Context Window]] of whichever model is doing the routing.
