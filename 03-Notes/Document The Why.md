## Principle

Record the reasoning behind a decision, not just the decision itself — visible in this vault's [[Decision — Vault Architecture v1]] note, which exists specifically to preserve why flat notes were chosen.

## Why

The decision itself is easy to rediscover by reading the code or the vault; the reasoning behind it is not. Without it, future-you (or future Claude) re-litigates settled questions instead of building on them.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Principles]]. This is what makes [[Constraints Before Features]] durable — a constraint without its reasoning recorded looks arbitrary and gets removed by someone who doesn't know why it was there.
