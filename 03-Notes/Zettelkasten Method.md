## Concept

A note-taking method built on small, single-idea notes that link densely to each other, letting structure emerge from connections rather than from a fixed folder hierarchy — the direct inspiration for this vault's flat `03-Notes/` rule.

Part of [[Hub — Knowledge Base]], and the reasoning behind both [[Atomic Notes]] and [[Hub and Spoke Structure]] — this vault's design is a Zettelkasten with hubs layered on top for navigation.
