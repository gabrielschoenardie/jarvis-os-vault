## Concept

Designing an assistant to be driven primarily by spoken input rather than typed commands or GUI navigation, which is the core interaction model of the jarvis-os app — voice as the primary channel, with visuals (like the Vault Brain) as a supporting, not primary, interface.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — JARVIS OS Core]], paired with [[Three.js Visualization]] as the visual complement to voice, and dependent on [[Hub — Prompt Engineering]] and [[Hub — AI Models]] to turn spoken requests into reliable actions.
