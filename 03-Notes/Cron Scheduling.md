## Concept

Running a task automatically on a fixed schedule (daily, weekly, every N minutes) rather than triggering it manually — the mechanism behind this vault's own Weekly Review habit described in [[Hub — Vault Maintenance]].

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Automation]], often used to drain a [[Task Queues]] on a regular cadence, and relevant to [[Agent Orchestration Basics]] when a scheduled job kicks off a multi-agent run.
