## Concept

Asking a model to work through intermediate reasoning steps before giving a final answer, which tends to improve accuracy on multi-step problems at the cost of more output tokens and a larger [[Model Context Window]] footprint.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Prompt Engineering]], often paired with [[System Prompt Design]] to instruct when reasoning should be shown, and complementary to [[Few-Shot Examples]] rather than a replacement for it.
