## Concept

x264 is the open-source encoder library that implements [[H.264]] encoding, and the one [[FFmpeg]] uses by default (`-c:v libx264`). Its tuning knobs — CRF, preset, maxrate/bufsize for [[VBV (Video Buffering Verifier)]] — are the actual levers behind almost every practical video-engineering decision in this vault.

## Connects to

x264 is the encoder at the center of [[Hub — Video Engineering]]: it produces [[H.264]] streams, is constrained by [[VBV (Video Buffering Verifier)]], receives color-graded input from [[LUTs (Look-Up Tables)]], and is evaluated afterward with [[VMAF]].
