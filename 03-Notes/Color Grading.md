## Concept

Adjusting the color and tone of an image or video after capture to achieve a consistent look — the still-photography counterpart of applying a [[LUTs (Look-Up Tables)]] to video footage, sharing the same underlying color-transform logic.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Photography]], applied after the [[Exposure Triangle]] has determined the raw capture, and mirrored in [[Hub — Video Engineering]] by [[LUTs (Look-Up Tables)]].
