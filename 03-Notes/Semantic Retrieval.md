## Concept

Finding relevant notes by meaning rather than exact keyword match, typically by comparing a query's [[Vector Embeddings]] against those of stored notes and returning the closest matches.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Memory System]], built on top of [[Vector Embeddings]], and complementary to [[Graph-Based Memory]] — this vault currently relies on graph links, but semantic retrieval is a natural extension as the note count grows.
