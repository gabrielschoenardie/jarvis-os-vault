## Concept

An atomic note captures exactly one idea, small enough to be linked precisely from many places without dragging unrelated content along with it — most notes in `03-Notes/`, including this one, follow this rule.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Knowledge Base]], a direct application of the [[Zettelkasten Method]], and what makes [[Hub and Spoke Structure]] work — hubs can only stay readable if the notes they link to are this small.
