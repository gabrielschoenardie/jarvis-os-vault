## Concept

An embedding maps a piece of text (a note, a query) to a point in a high-dimensional vector space such that semantically similar texts land near each other — the basis for [[Semantic Retrieval]] over a note collection too large to search by keyword alone.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — Memory System]], the input representation used by [[Semantic Retrieval]], and distinct from — but complementary to — [[Graph-Based Memory]], which captures explicit links rather than latent similarity.
