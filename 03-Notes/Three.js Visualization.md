## Concept

Three.js is the WebGL library used to render the jarvis-os Vault Brain in 3D — nodes for notes, edges for wikilinks, sized and colored by degree — accessed through [[React Three Fiber Rendering]] rather than called directly.

## Connects to

Part of [[Hub — JARVIS OS Core]], the rendering technology behind the [[Vault Brain Concept]], and built using the [[React Three Fiber Rendering]] pattern described in [[Hub — Architecture]].
